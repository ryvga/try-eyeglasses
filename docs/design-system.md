# Design System

## Direction

Flat paper-like utility, inspired by Extend.ai's clean grid, tactile buttons, mono labels, and restrained product-first layout.

## Tokens

- Background: warm off-white paper
- Borders: stone lines with stronger panel outlines
- Radius: 6-8px, never pill-heavy
- Buttons: mono uppercase labels, border-x, border-t, thicker bottom border, subtle pressed hover
- Typography: Geist Sans for UI, Geist Mono for labels and operational metadata

## UX Rules

- The first viewport must be the actual try-on workflow.
- Avoid a marketing-only hero.
- Keep controls obvious: photo, frame, generate, result.
- Keep SEO content below the studio and crawlable.
- Do not use decorative gradient orbs, dark sci-fi visuals, or fake stock-like hero imagery.
